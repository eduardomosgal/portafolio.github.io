About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary Product Designer who enjoys bringing structure to complexity. With experience in digital products and design systems, I focus on creating experiences that feel intuitive, accessible, and purposeful — not just visually appealing.

I care about structure. I care about usability. And I care about building things that actually make sense for the people using them and the teams behind them. Outside of work, I’m constantly curious. I like exploring how psychology, typography, and technology intersect, always looking for ways to make digital experiences feel more human.
