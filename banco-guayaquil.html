Overview

During my 6-month internship, I worked within the design team to strengthen the foundations of their digital product. My role was focused on the structural and documentation phase, ensuring that core components and patterns were not only visually correct but also scalable and well-organized.

The Context

In a large-scale financial environment, consistency is everything. I contributed by refining the Information Architecture and establishing clear documentation for foundations that would serve as the "source of truth" for designers and developers.

Strategic Research

Benchmarking

Analysis of industry standards and financial design patterns to ensure the system followed best practices of usability and trust.

Wireframing & IA

Structuring the flow of information and defining how core components would interact across different screens.

Documentation

Drafting guidelines for foundations (spacing, color, typography) to bridge the gap between design and engineering.

Refinement & Systematic Design

Beyond research, I executed high-precision technical tasks in Figma to improve the library's usability for the entire design team.

Refinement & Naming: Correcting visual bugs and implementing consistent naming for variants.

Auto Layout & Logic: Building components that are responsive and respect systematic spacing rules.

UI Implementation: Applying the system to design high-fidelity screens for specific user flows.

Reflection

Being part of a large-scale project taught me that design systems are 80% communication and 20% pixels. I learned how to contribute to a collective ecosystem, the value of rigorous documentation, and how a well-structured "foundation" can save hundreds of hours for a development team.
